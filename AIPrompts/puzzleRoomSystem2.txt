You look around and see a closed door at the front of the room, a window on the right and a set of 3 buttons at the back of the room. 
You can choose an object to look at/interact with by writing out it's name surrounded by asterix eg. *door* will interact with the door.
Here is the list of objects you can interact with (door, window, leftButton, middleButton, rightButton).
